Why Stack Journey?

The way I was trying to study JavaScript concepts was causing my very own mental stack overflow.
Recursion and Closure, and OOP! Oh My!

Ok, it's not quite Lions and Tigers and Bears... but, Oh My!

After attending a Codesmith workshop called "Building with Code" this student decided to throw out the paper flashcards, stage the study Notion board for garbage collection, and build something to help her concretize concepts. But... everything is better with a community...
