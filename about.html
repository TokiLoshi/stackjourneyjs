Why Stack Journey?

The way I was trying to study JavaScript concepts was causing my very own mental stack overflow.
Recursion and Closure, and OOP! Oh My!

Ok, it's not quite Lions and Tigers and Bears... but, Oh My!

On my mission to make friends with JavaScript and understand the concepts more deeply, I decided I should do that with code. So I'm throwing out the paper flashcards, staging the Notion board template I found for garbage collection, and spinning up a web app.
