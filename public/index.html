Code, community, and commit concepts

Learning to program is hard. StackJourney lets you keep track of concepts your group is struggling so you can help each other out. Get stuck and want to bring it up in your next study session? Push concepts onto your stack memory, so you and your group and run through it together. Pop concepts off of your group stack to accumulate points.
